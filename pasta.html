pasta with homemade tomato sauce

½ cup cashews, unsalted

2 large tomatoes, chopped

2 Tablespoons tomato paste

⅓ cup tomato sauce

¼ cup water

¼ teaspoon salt

¼ cup vegetable broth

2 Tablespoon extra virgin olive oil

2 garlic cloves, minced

fresh basil

Pasta of choice

Boil your pasta according to package directions. Place cashews, cut tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetable broth, salt, tomato sauce and water in a blender and puree until smooth. Heat a small saucepan with olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and let it cook for 1-2 minutes. Stir in cashew/tomato puree, and simmer. Taste and adjust for seasoning. Mix pasta and top with fresh basil.
