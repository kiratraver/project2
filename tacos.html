fried plantain and potato tacos

1 can black beans

2 plaintains, sliced

potatos, cubed

1 medium sweet onion

5 sticks of jicama

1 cup green and/or red cabbage

juice of 1 lime

1 tbsp agave

cilantro

flour tortilla

cube potatos and boil for 15-20 minutes, until easily pierced with fork. Once cooked, drain water. Heat oil over medium heat. Once heated, sautee potatos until crisp and browned. Remove from pan and add more oil, heating. Add sliced plantain and cook on both sides for 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Remove from pan, add more oil. sautee sliced onions until golden brown. add in black beans for 1-2 minutes until heated. In a medium size bowl, add sliced jicama and cabbage. Add 1 tsp olive oil, lime juice, agave, salt, and pepper.
